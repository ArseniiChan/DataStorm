🎯 Executive Summary

Our comprehensive analysis of MTA's Bus Automated Camera Enforcement (ACE) program reveals significant insights across three critical areas: CUNY student route performance, violation patterns, and congestion pricing effectiveness. Most notably, our data shows an 11.7% decrease in violations following the implementation of congestion pricing in Manhattan's Central Business District, demonstrating measurable policy success.
