🎯 Executive Summary

Our comprehensive analysis of MTA's Bus Automated Camera Enforcement (ACE) program reveals critical insights across three strategic areas: CUNY student route performance, violation exemption patterns, and congestion pricing effectiveness. Most significantly, our data demonstrates an 11.7% decrease in violations following Manhattan's congestion pricing implementation on January 5, 2025, providing concrete evidence of policy success and measurable traffic behavior change in the Central Business District.

📊 Question 1: CUNY Routes Speed Analysis
